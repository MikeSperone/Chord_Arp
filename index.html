Welcome to GitHub Pages.

This is a synthesizer that is all about chords. And not just like you would normally play on a guitar or keyboard. As a composer, I have come to love - and use often - a chordal texture where the notes fall randomly in each measure. And when you have a lot of of these randomly placed notes (I mean, a whole lot) a very beautiful texture emerges, playing the chords in a very nice way. I programmed this app to do just this, and it's easier to hear it to know what I'm talking about.
